# Geometric Product

## Definition

A **geometric product** between $\mathbf{a},\mathbf{b}\in \mathcal{V}_n$ where $\mathcal{V_n} \sube \mathbb{R}^n $ is given by:


$$
\mathbf{a}\mathbf{b} = \mathbf{a}\cdot\mathbf{b} + \mathbf{a}\wedge\mathbf{b} .
$$ 

> [!note]  
> The geometric product can be decomposed into symmetric (dot product) and antisymmetric (wedge product) parts.
