# Wedge Product

## Definition

The wedge product between two vectors $\mathbf{a},\mathbf{b}\in \mathcal{V}_n$ where $\mathcal{V_n} \sube \mathbb{R}^n $ is given by:

$$
\mathbf{a}\wedge\mathbf{b} = |a| |b| \underbrace{\mathbf{e_\mu}\mathbf{e_\nu}}_{=\mathbf{i}} \sin{\theta} 
$$ 

where $\mathbf{e_\mu}\perp\mathbf{e_\nu} \Rightarrow \mathbf{i}^2=-1$.

> [!note]
> The wedge product is the antisymmetric component of the geometric product.
