# Dot Product

## Definition

The dot product between two vectors $\mathbf{a},\mathbf{b}\in \mathcal{V}_n$ where $\mathcal{V_n} \sube \mathbb{R}^n $ is given by:

$$
\mathbf{a}\cdot\mathbf{b} = |a| |b| \cos{\theta} .
$$ 

> [!note]
> The dot product is the symmetric component of the geometric product.
